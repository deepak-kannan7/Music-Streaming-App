What is Autism?

  1. Autism, or autism spectrum disorder (ASD), refers to a broad range of conditions characterized by challenges with social skills, repetitive behaviors, speech and nonverbal communication.
  2. 1 in 44 children have autism in the US whereas 1 in 500 children are reported to have autism in India
  3. It is a serious developmental disorder that impairs the ability to communicate.

Use of Music Therapy

  1. Music Therapy for autism uses music to address physical, emotional, cognitive, social needs of children with autism. This can be done mostly by simulating the senses
  2. Music therapy increases the ability to associate sounds with communication of needs as it helps in connecting the auditory and motor functions of the brain.
  3. Similar to other forms of therapy, affiliations with music therapists to come and help a child with her/his therapy is encouraged. Apart from being a therapy in itself, Music therapy can go hand in hand with other therapies your child may be receiving.
